How this works

Word Blocker scans the title and (when visible) the description snippet of every video YouTube renders — on the home page, search results, channel pages, playlists, and the "Up next" sidebar — and compares them against your list, ignoring capitalization. Matching videos are blurred or hidden automatically, even as you scroll and more videos load in. Changes you save here apply instantly, no page reload needed.
